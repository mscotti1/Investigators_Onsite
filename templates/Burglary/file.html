The Roof Man

Summary: A suspect is under investigation for conducting a series of armed robberies at more than 30 McDonald's restaurants across the country. The audacious criminal strikes during early hours, displaying a weapon to intimidate employees and swiftly making off with cash from the registers. Law enforcement agencies are pooling resources, analyzing surveillance footage, and following leads to apprehend the elusive suspect, who has managed to evade capture thus far. The public is urged to remain vigilant, and authorities are working diligently to bring an end to the crime spree that has left the community shaken and concerned for public safety.
