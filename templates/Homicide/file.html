The Murder of Becky Faraday

  911 Operators recieved a call from Ronald Faraday reporting his wife, Becky Faraday's, dead body. The transcript is added below. When officers arrived on the scene, they found Becky Faraday's body it was cold and stiff with blood coagulated on her scalp, dried hard on her wrists, but still wet in the deeper pools. A fire captain and an EMS supervisor both concluded that she had died more than an hour earlier. An overview of the evidence found so far is attached.
